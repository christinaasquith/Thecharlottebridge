Corrections policy

We try our best, but mistakes happen. And unfortunately, "not only is it easy to be wrong on the internet, it's profitable", as Jeremy Arnold of Save Journalism says.

If we mess up, not only we will correct it- we'll make amends by buying you a $25 gift card to the county store of your choice: Carrie's Spear Street or Old Brick.

Please reach out in an email to all 5 cofounders about it. We'll get it corrected as soon as possible.
