The Charlotte Bridge Correction Policy


Accuracy and transparency are important to us. We try our best, but mistakes happen.

If you see something in a story that's just plain wrong, or if you feel like a story is misleading, one-sided, or otherwise contrary to our mission, please let us know.

If we mess up, we will correct it publicly. Furthermore, The Charlotte Bridge will make amends by buying you a $25 gift card to the country store of your choice: The Charlotte General Store (Carrie Spear's store) or Old Brick Store.

Please reach out to chea@thecharlottebridge.org, or email any or all of the cofounders (emails on homepage). We'll get it addressed as soon as possible.
